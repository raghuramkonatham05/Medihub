About MediHub

MediHub is an ML & AI-powered medical intelligence platform designed to help patients and healthcare professionals understand laboratory reports clearly, quickly, and confidently.

Medical lab reports often contain complex terminology, numerical ranges, and abbreviations that are difficult for non-medical users to interpret. MediHub bridges this gap by converting raw medical data into meaningful insights.

Using machine learning models trained on clinical patterns, MediHub detects abnormalities such as anemia, diabetes indicators, kidney-related issues, and common CBC irregularities.

The system integrates Smart Medical OCR to accurately extract data from scanned hospital PDFs and digital reports, ensuring real-world usability.

MediHub is built as a decision-support system and is not intended to replace certified medical professionals.
