About MediHub

MediHub is an AI-powered healthcare web platform designed to digitize, analyze, and interpret laboratory medical reports. It bridges the gap between raw lab data and meaningful health insights by combining Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Machine Learning, and rule-based medical analysis. Users can securely upload scanned or photographed lab reports (PDF or image), and MediHub automatically extracts patient details, lab values, detects abnormal results, identifies possible health risks, and presents everything in a professional medical dashboard. The platform is built with a strong focus on accuracy, privacy, and usability, making medical reports easier to understand for patients and helpful for preliminary clinical insights. MediHub does not replace doctors, but acts as a decision-support and awareness tool, helping users understand their health data better.
